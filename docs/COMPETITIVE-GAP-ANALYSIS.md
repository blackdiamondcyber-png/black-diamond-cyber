### Pricing Presentation
- No public pricing. Positions as premium consulting with a "book a call" flow.

### Trust Signals
- Enterprise client logos (Anthropic, Notion, Wix)
- Revenue figures on case studies ($90k/m, $2M/yr)
- Personal brand leverage (Nick's YouTube audience, Maker School community)

- **Revenue-metric case studies** are the single biggest gap. We need to show specific dollar outcomes.

- **Embedded calendar booking** eliminates friction vs. our modal-based contact form.

## Competitive Positioning Insight

Black Diamond Cyber has the strongest visual design and animation quality of any competitor analyzed. The dark luxe aesthetic, 3D tilt cards, magnetic CTAs, and Framer Motion animations are leagues ahead of every dental competitor (Gargle, DocSites, ProSites all use basic WordPress/Elementor templates). The gap is not in design sophistication -- it is in **trust signals, social proof, educational content, and interactive conversion tools**.

The dental competitors win on trust: association badges, video testimonials, hard metric case studies, and transparent pricing. The AI builders (Durable, 10Web) win on interactive demos and self-serve experiences. LeftClick wins on personal brand authority and enterprise social proof.

Our strategic advantage is that we can combine our superior visual execution with the trust/conversion elements that our competitors use. No dental competitor has anything close to our animation quality. If we layer on video testimonials, ROI calculators, case study metrics, and trust badges, we become the premium option that also proves its value.
